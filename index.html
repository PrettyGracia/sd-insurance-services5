Plant and Equipment Insurance

SD Insurance's Plant and Equipment Insurance is essential for businesses that rely on machinery and equipment for their operations. This insurance protects your business assets against damage, theft, and liability, ensuring that your business can continue to operate smoothly even in the event of an accident or other unforeseen circumstances. Whether you have a single piece of equipment or an entire fleet, our Plant and Equipment Insurance provides the coverage you need to keep your business running.

Key Features of Plant and Equipment Insurance

  • Comprehensive Coverage: Protection against damage to your plant and equipment caused by accidents, theft, fire, and other risks.
  • Third-Party Liability: Coverage for legal liability arising from injury or damage caused to third parties by your plant and equipment.
  • Breakdown Cover: Coverage for the cost of repairing or replacing equipment that breaks down unexpectedly.
  • Hire Costs: Coverage for the cost of hiring replacement equipment to minimize downtime and keep your business operations running smoothly.
  • Transit Cover: Protection for your plant and equipment while it is being transported between locations.
  • 24/7 Claims Support: Access to a dedicated claims team available around the clock to assist you in the event of an incident.
